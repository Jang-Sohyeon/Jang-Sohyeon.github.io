About Me

Hello, I am Sohyeon Jang.\nI am pursuing an M.S. degree in the Department of Artificial Intelligence Convergence at Myongji University, advised by Prof. Hyunhee Park in DAN Lab. I am currently in my second year and expect to graduate in February 2027.

Research Interests

Interests: Camouflaged Object Detection, Elusive Object Detection, Explainable AI (XAI), Dataset Analysis

I am interested in identifying why research progress stagnates and in defining problems more clearly.\nCurrently, I focus on objects that are visually similar to their backgrounds and difficult to detect. I analyze the structural causes of detection failure by examining data characteristics and how models interpret data.\nBased on this, I aim to contribute to improved dataset construction methods and detection model development.

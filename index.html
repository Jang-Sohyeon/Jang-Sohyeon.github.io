About Me

Hello, I am Sohyeon Jang.\nI am pursuing an M.S. degree in the Department of Artificial Intelligence Convergence at Myongji University, advised by Prof. Hyunhee Park in DAN Lab. I am currently in my third semester and expect to graduate in February 2027.

Research Interests

Interests: Camouflaged Object Detection (COD), Elusive Object Detection (EOD), Explainable AI (XAI)

I am interested in identifying why research progress becomes limited and in defining problems more clearly.\nCurrently, I focus on objects that are visually similar to their backgrounds and difficult to detect. I analyze why AI models miss specific objects by examining data characteristics, data distributions, and the way models interpret data. Through this process, I aim to understand the structural causes of detection failure.\nBased on these insights, I study experimental designs, difficulty analysis criteria, and data construction strategies that can reveal the problem more clearly.

About Me

Sohyeon Jang

M.S. Student · Applied Artificial Intelligence · Myongji University

Hello, I am Sohyeon Jang, an M.S. student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence Convergence at Myongji University. I am a member of DAN Lab, advised by Prof. Hyunhee Park. I am currently in my third semester and expect to graduate in February 2027.

Research Interests

My research focuses on visual AI problems involving objects that are difficult to distinguish from their surroundings, and on making model behavior more interpretable through explainable AI.

Camouflaged Object Detection

I study visual recognition problems where the object and background are highly similar.

CODVision AI

Elusive Object Detection

I am interested in detecting visually ambiguous objects that are hard to localize and recognize.

Elusive ObjectsDetection

Explainable AI (XAI)

I explore ways to understand where visual models look and why they make certain predictions.

XAIInterpretability
